CET full form: What is the full form of CET Exam

The CET full form is Common Entrance Text.This exam is conducted by NRA (National Needs Agency) all over India to get admission in professional courses like Doctorate, Dental, and Engineering in state-level vocational colleges. Earlier, to get admission in SSC, Railways, or IBPS, different exams had to be given, due to which students had to face a lot of difficulties in preparing for the exam. But, Common Entrance Test allows students to take only one entrance test to take admission in various professional courses.
